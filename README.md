### Desalination Plants Performance Analysis Dashboard
This project presents a comprehensive Power BI analytics solution designed to evaluate the operational, financial, and sustainability performance of 71 desalination plants across three Gulf countries: Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), and Qatar. The dataset was generated for analytical and demonstration purposes to showcase advanced business intelligence, data visualization, and reporting capabilities.
The dashboard transforms complex operational data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor plant performance, compare operational efficiency across countries, and support data-driven decision-making within the water desalination sector.

Business Objective:
The primary objective of this dashboard is to help decision-makers:
- Monitor desalination plant performance across multiple countries
- Analyze production capacity and storage utilization
- Evaluate workforce distribution and operational resources
- Assess transportation costs and logistics efficiency
- Measure financial performance through revenue, expenses, and profitability indicators
- Analyze energy efficiency and environmental sustainability metrics
- Identify opportunities for operational optimization and cost reduction
